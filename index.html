Redefining Junk

Artists Crystal Jerew and Kathy Qua married their deep love of vintage furniture with their creative talents. They upcycle hidden treasure from thrift stores and yard sales into custom finish pieces. Whether it be custom painting furniture with chalk paints or dressing up lamps long forgotten in the attic, no project is too small for this duo of best friend artists. Funky junk uses imagination and creativity to repurpose what was once "junk" into unique, timeless furniture.

Our greatest love is hunting for hidden treasures in thrift stores, Good-will and yard sales. We have decided to take our deep love of all things "junk" and marry it with our creative talents...breathing new life into previously owned items and refinishing them with a little bit of sass!

Whether it be custom painting furniture with chalk paints and beautiful finishes or dressing up lamps long forgotten in the attic, no project is too small! We especially love all things vintage. Using our imagination to repurpose and upcycle found treasures and selling them so they can live on is the focal part of our business.
